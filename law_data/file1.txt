Companies Act, 2013
Governs the incorporation, regulation, and dissolution of companies in India, ensuring transparency, accountability, and good governance practices for businesses.

Constitution of India
The supreme law of India, laying down the framework of political principles, procedures, and powers of government institutions and basic rights of citizens.

Consumer Protection Act, 2019
Protects the rights of consumers, providing for the establishment of consumer courts to settle disputes and address grievances regarding goods and services.

Criminal Procedure Code, 1973 (CrPC)
Provides the procedure for the investigation, prosecution, and trial of criminal offenses, detailing the rights of the accused and the procedure for criminal justice.

Indian Evidence Act, 1872
Sets the rules for the admissibility, relevance, and reliability of evidence in both civil and criminal cases, ensuring fair trials.

Indian Penal Code, 1860 (IPC)
The primary criminal code of India, defining various criminal offenses and penalties, as well as procedures for prosecution and punishment.
